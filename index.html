Work Experience

Bull 18

After my graduation I joined Bull 18 which is an up and coming company in web services market. I worked there as a customer service representative.

Key responsibilites
  • Talking to customers and holding meetings discussing their needs and requirements.
  • Keeping key stakeholders upto date with current progress of the project with regular meetings and presentations.
  • Helping IT team in understanding client requirements.
  • Delivering product to customer and taking feedback.
    • What I learned there

    By being in a place where everything was always changing and pivoting, it made me great at adapting to new things much more quickly and flexible to change. Interacting with customers was my major role there, and dealing with various customers with different and niche needs. I learnt how to handle customers, give status updates and presentation. Translating user requirements into what the IT team can understand and work on. And translating the technical jargon to what the customer and other stakeholders can understand. What I consider one of the most valuable learning I got from working there was how to hand an angry customer. I consider it one my most valuable learning because through it I learned how to have patience while dealing with difficult customers, work through the problem the customer is facing or why are they dissatisfied and how we as a team can deliver the product that meets customers expectation. Also how to set the customers expectations to be realistic in the first place.

But over the years I have learned the art of delegation, seeing through people and gauging what they’re capable of doing and delivering, delegating work accordingly to get desired results from them.
